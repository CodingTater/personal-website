Automated Aquaponics

Aquaponics, simply put is growing plants and fish in a closed loop symbiotically. To meet our ever increasing demand on our planets resources and ariable land I belive this is the farming of the future due to the tremendous potential to produce outsized yields based on relatively little resource input and space requirements. I am very interested in learning how to monitor and automate the process, carefully controlling every aspect of both the fish and plants growth cycles. I believe this can be done combining JavaScript with hardware. Additionally I want to make all the data and information I will naturally be collecting freely available to my consumers enabling people to know exactly what is in their food.

Art Du Jour

I want to create a membership type site to bring local artists and art lovers together with a "art-of-the-month" type of arrangement. The idea is to allow consumers to rent pieces of art to decorate their houses for a season/month/special occasion. They would pay a memebership fee and be able to swap out pieces as their tastes change. This solves the problems faced by art consumers of haivng to pay large sums to purchase static pieces of art that they may or may not always want to display. Additionally it allows for member artists to gain noteriaty for their work while generating a more consistent stream of income from renting out their pieces. Further down the road I would like to explore predictive analytics and utilize micro-expression technologies to better help connect art lovers to pieces they would be more apt to appreciate.
